PyTIS Homepage¶

Introduction¶

PyTIS is work in progress. It works well for the features that are implemented, but at the time of writing, only a few things are implemented. The idea is to gradually extend PyTIS that it can be used for transition interface sampling, or other extended ensemble methods.
